_______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Q=  What is === operator? 
_______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
A=The strict equality operator ( === ) checks whether its two operands are equal, returning a Boolean result. Unlike the equality operator, the strict equality operator always considers operands of different types to be different.
